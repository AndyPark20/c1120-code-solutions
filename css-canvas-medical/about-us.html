What we Believe

Why did we enter this crowded field? Because medical records have fallen dramatically short of their promise to improve the quality and efficiency of health care. Clinicians spend far too long at their computers. Workflows are overly restrictive. Valuable medical data is left unstructured.
We are honred, and humbled, when a practicee chooses us as their technology partner. Medicine is a delicate craft that mixes art and science- technology can help or hinder this craft. Working with clinicians across the country, we think we have begun to build something that helps.
